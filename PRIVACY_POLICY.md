# Privacy Policy for SubManager

**Effective Date:** April 19, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 19, 2026

Welcome to **SubManager** ("we", "our", or "us"). SubManager is a subscription management application designed to help users track their recurring payments and service usage. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information:** When you register, we collect your name, email address, and authentication data (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).
- **Subscription Data:** Details about your subscriptions (name, cost, billing cycle, renewal dates) that you manually enter into the app.
- **Profile Information:** Display name and profile picture (if imported via Google Sign-In).

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage Data:** Basic app usage information (e.g., features used, frequency of use) to improve app performance and stability.
- **Device Information:** Information about the mobile device you use to access the App, such as the hardware model and operating system version.

### 1.3 Information from Third-Party Services
- **Google Sign-In:** If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email from Google, subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Firebase Services:** We use Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore, subject to [Google Firebase's Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Data Used |
|---------|-----------|
| Create and manage your account | Email, Name |
| Track and manage subscriptions | Subscription names, costs, dates |
| Send renewal notifications | Device notification tokens |
| Analyze spending patterns | Aggregated subscription data |
| Authenticate your identity | Firebase Auth tokens |
| Improve app functionality | Anonymized usage data |

We **do not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

---

## 3. Device Permissions
The App requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To send you alerts before your subscriptions renew. |
| `INTERNET` | To sync your data with Firebase and enable cloud backup. |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | To ensure notifications are rescheduled after a device restart. |

You may revoke these permissions at any time via your device settings.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Security
- Your data is stored securely on **Google Cloud Firestore**.
- All data in transit is protected using industry-standard HTTPS encryption.
- We implement strict access controls to ensure your personal information remains confidential.

---

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion
- **Retention:** We retain your information for as long as your account is active.
- **Deletion:** You can delete your account and all associated subscription data directly from the App settings. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy
SubManager is **not directed to children under the age of 13**. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App and on our GitHub repository. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
